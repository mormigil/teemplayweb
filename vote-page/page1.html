Zealot's Forge

Description:

A 1st Person, team-centered action/building hybrid game that mixes the building functionality of Minecraft with the fast-paced, goal-driven gameplay of League of Legends.

Players compete against each other in 5v5 matches in order to destroy enemy structures, build bulwark defenses, kill other players, and win the game by destroying the enemy Obelisk. Players choose a unique hero before each match in the pre-game lobby, each with a different set of skills and characteristics, allowing for a different team composition and game experience every match.

Matches have two phases: Preparation Phase, and Engagement Phase.

The idea is to create a competitive game that is fun to play, and fun to watch being played. Zealot's Forge takes the unique building elements from Minecraft that make every match different, and mixes them with goal-driven gameplay, similiar to League of Legends and Team Fortress 2.
