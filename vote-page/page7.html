The Alchemist

Description:

The Alchemist is a game focused on the joy of discovery and understanding, where players break down potions and metals, in search of finding the fundamental substance.

The Alchemist is an explorative game in the vein of “The Sandbox” and “Lil Inferno,” where players are given a variety of potions, as well as means to manipulate them, such as boiling, combining, freezing, etc. Through experimentation, the player will discover more substances and more ways of manipulating them. The ultimate goal is to find the “Fundamental Substance” as well as finding fun Easter Eggs and novelty potions.
