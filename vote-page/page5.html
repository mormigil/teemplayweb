Unwind

Description:

Unwind is a puzzle game that will have players retrace the history of a young time-traveler through an immersive and detailed game.

The time-traveler is a boy with a righteous heart that wants to correct the problems of humanity. A disastrous event leaves the time-traveler with amnesia, searching for his long-lost memories.

Each person he interacts with is a new puzzle to solve as he tries to piece his life back together; a journal full of stories being his only memento. Every segment re-lived brings him closer to finding out the truth behind his lost past.
